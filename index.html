Who am I?

I'll avoid to answer this daunting question and say what you  are probably looking for:
I am an PhD student (2023 - 2026) at Université Paul Sabatier (UPS), under the supervision of Vincent Guedj, from in São Gonçalo-RJ-Brazil. My main interest in mathematics revolve around Differential Geometry. More specifically, my current research focus on Hermitian Geometry, Canonical Metrics, Complex Monge-Ampère Equation and Pluripotential Theory.

To contact me about corrections/suggestions on my notes or pretty much anything else: guicerqg at gmail.com

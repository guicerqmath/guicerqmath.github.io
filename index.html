Who am I?

I'll avoid to answer this dauting question and say what you  are probably looking for:
I am an undergraduate student at University of São Paulo (USP), born in Niterói-RJ-Brazil. My main interest in mathematics revolve around Differential Geometry. More specifically, my Undergraduate Thesis is about results on Geodesics in Mechanics and Finsler Geometry with an introduction to Finsler Transnormal Foliations, my advisor was Marcos Alexandrino. 

To contact me about corrections/suggestions on my notes or pretty much anything else: guicerqg at gmail.com
